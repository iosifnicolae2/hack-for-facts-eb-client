## Entity

An institution such as a city hall, county council, or ministry.

## UAT / Județ

UAT: local administrative unit (municipality/town/commune). Județ: county.

## Expenses (Cheltuieli) / Revenues (Venituri)

Two sides of a budget: spending vs income.

## Functional / Economic classification

Official categories describing what money is used for and its economic nature (e.g., Education, Goods & Services).

## Per Capita / Total

Normalized by population vs raw totals.

## Chart series

Individual data lines/bars you add to a chart. They can be dynamic (aggregated line items), calculated, static datasets, or custom values.
