You choose what’s shared. Transparenta.eu works with essential cookies only. With your permission, it also enables analytics and enhanced error reporting to improve the product. You can change this anytime.

### Quick Start
1) Open transparenta.eu
2) On first visit, a “Cookie settings” banner appears
3) Click “Essential only” to keep analytics off
4) Or click “Accept all” to enable analytics and enhanced errors
5) Click “Cookie Settings” to review later
6) You can export/share data regardless of your choice

### Understanding the Numbers
- Cookies vs data
  - Cookies control optional telemetry, not public data access
- Per Capita and Totals
  - Numbers don’t change with cookie choices
- Sources
  - Budget data remains the same for all users

### Troubleshooting & FAQ
- The banner came back
  - Your browser cleared storage. Choose again
- I clicked the wrong option
  - Open the Cookies page and change it
- Does “Essential only” limit features?
  - No. All data tools remain available
- Is my data tracked?
  - Only if you “Accept all”. Otherwise, essential only

### Accessibility & Keyboard tips
