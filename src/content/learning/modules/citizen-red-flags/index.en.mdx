## Introduction

### Patterns That Matter

Budget problems leave traces. Once you know what to look for, patterns emerge. This module teaches you to recognize warning signs.

> **Key insight:** A single red flag isn't proof of wrongdoing โ but multiple red flags together warrant investigation.
