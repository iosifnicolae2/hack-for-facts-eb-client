# Anatomy of Romanian Public Finance

Master the structure of public money flows.

## The Consolidated General Budget

The Consolidated General Budget represents about 40-44% of GDP and includes:
- **State Budget**: Ministries, central agencies, transfers.
- **Local Budgets**: ~3,180 units (counties, municipalities, towns, communes).
- **Social Insurance Budget (BASS)**: Pensions, unemployment.
- **Health Fund (FNUASS)**: Hospitals, drugs.
- **Special Funds**: Road fund, environment fund, etc.

## Key actors and documents

| Actor | Role | Documents for investigation |
|-------|------|----------------------------|
| Ministry of Finance | Coordinates, consolidates | Budget laws, execution reports, fiscal strategy |
| Line ministries | Sectoral spending | Own budgets, procurement, activity reports |
| Local governments | ~3,180 autonomous budgets | Local budgets, HCL decisions |
| Curtea de Conturi | Post-hoc audit | Annual reports, thematic audits |
