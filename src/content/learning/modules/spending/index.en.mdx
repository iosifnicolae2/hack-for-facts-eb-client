# Public Spending

Once collected, public money is spent according to the budget approved by Parliament.

## Main categories of spending

### 1. Personnel costs
- Salaries for public servants
- Social contributions
- Bonuses and allowances

### 2. Goods and services
- Utilities (water, electricity, gas)
- Supplies and materials
- Maintenance services

### 3. Investments
- New construction (schools, hospitals, roads)
- Modernizations
- Equipment purchases

### 4. Transfers
- Subsidies for state companies
- Social assistance
- Pensions and benefits

## How is spending controlled?

Every public institution must:
1. Respect approved budget limits
2. Report spending periodically
3. Justify every payment with documentation
