## Introduction

### Think Global, Act Local

The national budget gets the headlines, but local budgets often have more direct impact on your daily life. Schools, roads, parks, water â€” these are usually local responsibilities.

> **Key insight:** Local budgets are easier to monitor, understand, and influence than national ones. Start here.
