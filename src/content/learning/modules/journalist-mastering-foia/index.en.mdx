## Introduction

### Your Legal Right to Information

> **Key Insight:** FOIA is not asking for a favor â€” it's exercising a legal right. Law 544/2001 guarantees access to public information. Knowing how to use it effectively is your superpower.

Most journalists accept the first "no" or never follow up on delays. Persistence, combined with proper technique, gets results.
