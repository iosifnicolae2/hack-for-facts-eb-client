## Introduction

### Planning Is Where Compliance Begins

Budget preparation is not just an administrative exercise—it sets the trajectory for the entire fiscal year. A well-prepared budget prevents mid-year crises, audit findings, and the need for emergency rectifications.

> **Key Insight:** The budget calendar was significantly updated by Legea 270/2013. Using outdated deadlines (like "October 15 submission") is a compliance risk. The current deadline for OPC proposals is **September 1-15**.

## Platform Integration: Benchmark Your Planning

### Guided Activity: Compare Execution Rates

Use Transparenta.eu to understand how similar entities execute their budgets—this informs realistic planning.

> **Try it on Transparenta.eu:**
> 1. Open [Entity Analytics](/entity-analytics)
> 2. Search for your institution
> 3. Add 2-3 peer institutions (similar size, sector)
> 4. Compare execution rates by category (personnel, goods/services, capital)
> 5. Note: If peers execute 95% and you execute 70%, your planning may need adjustment
