# Explore Your Local Budget

## Concept Introduction

Now that you understand how local governments are structured and funded, it's time to find YOUR local budget. Whether you live in a major city or a small village, your UAT has budget data available on Transparenta.eu.

Local budgets are the best place to start your budget monitoring journey. They're smaller, more understandable, and the decision-makers â€” your mayor and local council â€” are accessible. You can actually attend council meetings and ask questions.

> **Key Insight:** Start local, build skills, then scale up. Local budgets are easier to monitor, understand, and influence than national ones.

## Why Start Local?

Local budgets offer unique advantages for citizen oversight:

| Aspect | National Budget | Local Budget |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|
| **Scale** | ~600B RON | ~2B RON (typical city) |
| | Hard to comprehend | Manageable |
| **Decision makers** | PM, Ministers | Mayor, Local Council |
| | Hard to reach | Accessible |
| **Meetings** | Parliament | Local council |
| | Can't attend | Open to public |
| **Direct impact** | Indirect | Roads, schools, parks |
