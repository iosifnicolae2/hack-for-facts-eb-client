# Your Three Roles

## Concept Introduction

You don't have just one relationship with public budgets â€” you have three. Most people only think of themselves as taxpayers (the money going out), but you're equally a beneficiary (services coming in) and a watchdog (your democratic right to oversight).

Understanding all three roles empowers you to engage more meaningfully with how public money is managed.

> **Key Insight:** Every citizen simultaneously pays into the system, benefits from it, and has the legal right to scrutinize how it operates.

Every purchase you make includes VAT (19% standard rate). Every paycheck has income tax and social contributions deducted. In total, the average Romanian worker contributes roughly 35% of their gross salary to the state.

This money funds everything from your local road repairs to national defense.

Don't just think about what you pay â€” think about what you receive:

Most citizens receive more in services than they pay in taxes â€” that's how progressive taxation and public goods work.

This might be your most important role. **Law 544/2001** (Legea privind liberul acces la informaÈ›iile de interes public) guarantees your right to request any public information from any public institution.
