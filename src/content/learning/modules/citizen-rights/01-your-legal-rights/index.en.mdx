## Core Explanation

### Law 544/2001: Your Right to Know

This is Romania's Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). It gives you the legal right to request any document of public interest from any public institution.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Response Time** | 10 working days (standard) |
| **Extended Time** | 30 days for complex requests |
| **Cost** | Free (or minimal copying costs) |
| **Appeal** | Yesâ€”administrative, then court |
| **Penalties** | Fines for non-compliance |

**What you can request:**
- Budget execution reports
- Public contracts and procurement files
- Salary grids and organizational charts
- Meeting minutes and council decisions

### Law 52/2003: Your Right to Participate

This law ensures you can participate *before* decisions are made, not just react afterward.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Draft Publication** | Must be published for comment |
| **Comment Period** | Minimum 30 days |
| **Public Meetings** | Right to attend and speak |
| **Feedback** | Must be officially considered |

**When it applies:**
- Budget proposals and rectifications
- New local regulations
- Urban planning decisions
- Any decision affecting citizens
