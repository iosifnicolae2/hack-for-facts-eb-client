## Core Explanation

### Law 544/2001: Your Right to Know

This is Romania's Freedom of Information Act (FOIA). It gives you the legal right to request any document of public interest from any public institution.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Response Time** | 10 working days (standard) |
| **Extended Time** | 30 days for complex requests |
| **Refusal Deadline** | 5 days to communicate a refusal |
| **Media Requests** | 24 hours for verbal requests |
| **Cost** | Free (court appeals exempt from fees) |
| **Appeal** | Administrative or directly to court |
| **Liability** | Disciplinary action for officials |

**What you can request (explicitly covered by Art. 5):**
- Budget execution reports and financial statements
- Public contracts and procurement files
- Organizational charts
- Council decisions and normative acts

> **Note:** Salary grids and meeting minutes are not explicitly listed in Art. 5 and may face exceptions under Art. 12 (deliberations, personal data).

### Law 52/2003: Your Right to Participate

This law ensures you can participate *before* decisions are made, not just react afterward.

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Advance Notice** | 30 working days before approval |
| **Comment Period** | Minimum 10 calendar days |
| **Draft Publication** | Website, HQ display, media, stakeholders |
| **Public Meetings** | Right to attend (3-day advance notice) |
| **Feedback Response** | Authority must justify rejected comments |

**When it applies:**
- Budget proposals and rectifications
- New local regulations
- Urban planning decisions
- Any decision affecting citizens
