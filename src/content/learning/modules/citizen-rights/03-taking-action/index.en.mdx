# Taking Action: Engagement and Reporting

## Concept Introduction

Knowing your rights is just the first step. Real impact comes from taking actionâ€”whether that's monitoring budgets, attending public meetings, or reporting problems to the right authorities.

Civic engagement isn't all-or-nothing. There's a ladder of involvement, from low-effort monitoring to high-impact reporting. Start where you're comfortable, and build up over time.

> **Key Insight:** You don't need to become a full-time activist. Even setting up budget alerts on Transparenta.eu counts as civic engagement. The key is consistencyâ€”stay informed, and escalate when you find something wrong.

### Before You Report

**Document everything:**
- Screenshot relevant data from Transparenta.eu
- Save budget documents and reports
- Note dates, amounts, and specific discrepancies
- Keep copies of any FOIA requests and responses

**Be specific:**
- Reference exact figures and classifications
- Explain what the problem is and why it matters
- Provide evidence, not just suspicions
