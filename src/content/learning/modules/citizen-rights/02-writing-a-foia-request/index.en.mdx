# Writing a FOIA Request

## Concept Introduction

Now that you know your legal rights, it's time to use them. A FOIA (Freedom of Information Act) request under Law 544/2001 is your most powerful tool for getting specific budget information.

The good news? It's simpler than you think. A well-crafted request takes 10 minutes to write and can unlock data that would otherwise be impossible to find.

> **Key Insight:** Be specific. "Tell me about your budget" is weak. "Provide the 2024 budget execution report for functional classification 65.02 (Education)" is powerfulâ€”it shows you know exactly what you want.
