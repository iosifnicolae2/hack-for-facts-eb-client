## Platform Integration: Guided Activity

### Activity: Write Your Story Lead

**Step 1: Gather Your Key Facts**

From your investigation, identify:
- The main finding (one sentence)
- The scale (amount, percentage, comparison)
- The impact (who is affected)
- The players (who is responsible)

**Step 2: Write Three Lead Options**

Write the opening paragraph three ways:

1. **Data-first lead**: Start with the most striking number
2. **Person-first lead**: Start with an affected individual
3. **Discovery-first lead**: Start with what the investigation found

**Step 3: Test with Non-Expert Reader**

Show your leads to someone unfamiliar with budget data:
- Do they understand what happened?
- Do they understand why it matters?
- Which version is clearest?

**Step 4: Refine and Expand**

Using feedback, write the full opening section:
- Lead paragraph (what happened)
- Nut graf (key finding in context)
- Bridge to evidence (why this matters)
