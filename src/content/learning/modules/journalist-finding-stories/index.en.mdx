### Components Needed

3. **OutcomeConnectionMapper** - Spending-outcome visualization
4. **HypothesisGenerator** - Structured hypothesis builder
5. **StoryDiscoveryExercise** - Multi-step guided activity

### Data Requirements

- Entity comparison data (API)
- Statistical calculations (mean, std dev)
- Outcome data sources (links/embedded)
- SICAP integration for triangulation

- Education: Ministry of Education test scores
- Health: Ministry of Health statistics
- Crime: Police statistics
- Infrastructure: Road quality indexes

- Method cards keyboard navigable
- Comparison tables have row/column headers
- Exercise progress tracked and announced
- Hypothesis form inputs properly labeled
