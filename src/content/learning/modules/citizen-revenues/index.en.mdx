## Introduction

### Where Does Your Money Go?

Every time you receive a salary, buy something, or own property, part of that money goes to the state. But how much exactly? And where does it end up?

> **Fun fact:** The average Romanian worker contributes about **45%** of their labor cost to the state through various taxes and contributions â€” but most people don't realize it because it's split between employee and employer.

### Content for Each Section

**Taxes Paid by Businesses:**
- Corporate income tax: 16%
- Micro-enterprise tax: 1-3% (based on employees)
- VAT: 19% standard, 9% food/tourism, 5% books/housing

**Social Contributions:**
- CAS (pension): 25% employee
- CASS (health): 10% employee
- CAM (work insurance): 2.25% employer

**EU Funds:**
- Structural funds
- Cohesion Fund
- PNRR (Recovery and Resilience)
- Require 15-25% national co-financing

**Other Sources:**
- State-owned enterprise dividends
- Fees, fines, concessions
- Public debt (borrowing)

- Charts have text alternatives

- Color is not the only indicator (add icons/patterns)
