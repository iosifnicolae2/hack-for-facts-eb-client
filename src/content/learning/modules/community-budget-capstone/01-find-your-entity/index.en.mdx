## How to Find Your Entity

Follow these steps to locate your local government:

1. **Open Entity Analytics** — Click the link or navigate to the Entities section
2. **Search by name** — Type your city, town, or commune name
3. **Verify the entity** — Check the county matches your location
4. **Note the CUI** — This unique identifier helps track the entity

### What Entity Types Mean

| Type | Romanian Term | Description |
|------|---------------|-------------|
| Municipality | Municipiu | Large city (county capital or major urban center) |
| Town | Oras | Medium-sized urban area |
| Commune | Comuna | Rural administrative unit (villages) |

> **Tip:** If your village name doesn't appear, search for the commune that administers it. Villages are part of communes in Romania's administrative structure.

## Why Population Matters

Population is crucial for meaningful budget comparisons:

- **Per capita spending** = Total Budget ÷ Population
- Comparing raw totals is misleading (Bucharest vs. a small commune)
- Per capita figures reveal spending intensity and priorities

**Example:**
- City A: 100 million RON budget, 100,000 population → 1,000 RON per capita
- City B: 10 million RON budget, 5,000 population → 2,000 RON per capita

City B actually spends more per person despite the smaller total!
