### Activity: Track EU Fund Execution in Your Region

**Step 1: Find EU-Funded Entities**

Navigate to Transparenta.eu Budget Explorer:
```
/budget-explorer?accountCategory=ch&economicCode=58
```

Look for entities with significant EU fund spending.

**Step 2: Analyze EU vs National Spending**

For a selected entity, compare:
- Code 58.* (EU funds)
- Other spending codes

Questions to investigate:
- What percentage of total budget comes from EU funds?
- Is execution rate different for EU vs national funds?
- Are there Code 58 allocations with 0% execution?

**Step 3: Cross-Reference with Project Data**

For entities with high EU fund spending:
1. Search MySMIS for their projects
2. Match budget lines with project allocations
3. Identify discrepancies
