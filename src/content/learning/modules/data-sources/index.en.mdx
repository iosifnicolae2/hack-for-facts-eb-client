# Primary Data Sources

Know where every piece of budget data lives.

## Official publications

| Source | URL | What you get |
|--------|-----|--------------|
| Monitorul Oficial | monitoruloficial.ro | Budget laws, HG, OUG |
| Ministry of Finance | mfinante.gov.ro | National execution, fiscal reports |
| SEAP/SICAP | e-licitatie.ro | All public procurement |
| Curtea de Conturi | curteadeconturi.ro | Audit reports |

## Internal documents (requires FOIA)

- **Notele de fundamentare** (justification memos)
- Internal audit reports
- Contract annexes and amendments
- Payment orders and invoices
