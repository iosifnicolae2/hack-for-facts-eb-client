# Two Lenses on Spending

## Concept Introduction

When you look at government spending, you can ask two very different questions:

1. **"What is the money FOR?"** â†’ Education, Health, Defense, etc.
2. **"What TYPE of spending is it?"** â†’ Salaries, Equipment, Buildings, etc.

These two questions give you two different views of the same money. Romania's budget uses both systems, and understanding the difference unlocks powerful insights.

> **Key Insight:** The same 10 million RON can be "Education spending" (functional) AND "Salary payments" (economic) at the same time. They're not competing categories â€” they're different lenses on the same reality.

## Core Explanation: The Two Classifications

### Functional Classification ("What for?")

Answers: **What purpose does this money serve?**

| Code | Category | Question it Answers |
|------|----------|---------------------|
| 65 | Education | How much goes to schools? |
| 66 | Health | How much goes to hospitals? |
| 68 | Social Protection | How much goes to pensions? |
| 60 | Defense | How much goes to military? |

This is what most citizens think of when they think about "the budget." It tells you which sectors receive funding.

### Economic Classification ("What type?")

Answers: **What is the nature of the expense?**

| Code | Category | What it Includes |
|------|----------|------------------|
| 10 | Personnel | Salaries, bonuses, social contributions |
| 20 | Goods & Services | Utilities, supplies, maintenance, contracts |
| 50-59 | Transfers | Subsidies, grants, pensions, welfare |
| 71 | Capital Investments | Buildings, roads, equipment, vehicles |
| 80 | Debt Service | Interest payments, loan repayments |

This reveals how money is spent regardless of sector. It's crucial for understanding the structure of government spending.

### Why Both Matter

Consider this question: **"How much does Romania spend on teacher salaries?"**

To answer, you need BOTH classifications:
- **Functional:** Filter to Education (code 65)
- **Economic:** Filter to Personnel (code 10)
- **Result:** Education + Personnel = Teacher salaries

> **Pro Tip:** When investigating spending, always ask both questions. "Health spending increased" could mean more nurses (personnel) OR more medical equipment (investments). The economic breakdown tells you which.
