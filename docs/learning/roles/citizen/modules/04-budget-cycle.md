### Phase Details (Expanded Content)

**1. PLANNING (May-August)**
- Ministry of Finance sets spending ceilings
- Each institution prepares budget requests
- Internal negotiations happen
- ğŸ¯ **Your opportunity:** Limited â€” internal process

**2. DRAFTING (September - October 15)**
- Government consolidates all requests
- Budget draft prepared
- **Legal deadline: October 15** â€” must submit to Parliament
- ğŸ¯ **Your opportunity:** Public consultations may occur

**3. APPROVAL (October-December)**
- Parliament debates the budget
- Amendments proposed and voted
- Final vote usually in December
- ğŸ¯ **Your opportunity:** HIGH â€” contact MPs, attend sessions

**4. EXECUTION (January-December)**
- Institutions spend according to approved budget
- Quarterly reports published
- Rectifications may occur
- ğŸ¯ **Your opportunity:** Monitor execution rates

**5. REPORTING (Quarterly + Annual)**
- Execution reports made public
- Comparison: planned vs. actual
- Available on Forexebug and ministry sites
- ğŸ¯ **Your opportunity:** Analyze and question

**6. AUDIT (Following Year)**
- Court of Accounts reviews spending
- Reports identify irregularities
- Recommendations issued
- ğŸ¯ **Your opportunity:** Read audit reports, follow up
