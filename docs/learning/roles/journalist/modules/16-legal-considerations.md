## Platform Integration: Guided Activity

### Activity: Assess Legal Risk of Your Investigation

**Step 1: Apply the Risk Matrix**

For your current investigation, score each dimension:
- Who is the subject? (public vs. private figure)
- What are you claiming? (severity of allegations)
- What is your evidence? (documents vs. sources)
- How are you presenting it? (facts vs. accusations)
- Did you offer right of reply? (timing and method)

**Step 2: Identify Weak Points**

Which dimension scored highest risk?
- If evidence is weak: Consider delaying for more documentation
- If presentation is risky: Review language with editor
- If right of reply missing: Send before publication

**Step 3: Build Defense Package**

Using the Document Defense Builder template:
- List all primary source documents
- Gather right of reply documentation
- Document your methodology

**Step 4: Seek Review if Needed**

For high-risk stories:
- Contact CJI or ActiveWatch for pre-publication review
- Brief your editor on risk assessment
- Consider legal consultation before publication
