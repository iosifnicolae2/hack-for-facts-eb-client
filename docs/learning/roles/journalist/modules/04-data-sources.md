## Introduction

### Your Data Arsenal

> **Key Insight:** Layer your sources: official publications for legitimacy, structured systems for data, aggregators for analysis, and internal documents (via FOIA) for the "why" behind the numbers.

Budget data exists in multiple places, each with different levels of detail, accessibility, and reliability. Mastering this landscape is essential for effective investigation.
