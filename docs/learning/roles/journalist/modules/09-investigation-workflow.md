## Introduction

### From Lead to Publication

> **Key Insight:** Investigations have phases. Rushing from discovery to publication without proper verification is how journalists lose credibility and lawsuits.

A structured workflow protects your story, your sources, and yourself. This module gives you a systematic approach to move from initial lead to published investigation.

- Email integration for right of reply

- Progress indicators announced
- Checklists keyboard navigable
