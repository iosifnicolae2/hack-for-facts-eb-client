## Introduction

### Recognizing Warning Signs

> **Key Insight:** Red flags are starting points, not conclusions. They tell you where to look, not what you'll find.

Budget data contains patterns that experienced investigators recognize as potential problems. Learning these patterns accelerates your ability to find stories.
